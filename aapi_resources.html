Anti Asian American and Pacific Islander Hate

Anti AAPI Hate is an organization whose goal is to build a multiracial movement to fight against systemic racism and establish equity. This website provides mental health and educational resources for educators and individuals. It also allows viewers to report incidents against AAPI, and shares data collected about these reports.

OCA-Asian Pacific American Advocates

OCA-Asian Pacific advocates for AAPI civil rights. Their mission is to advance the social, political, and economic well-being of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders in the United States. Partipate in OCA-Asian Pacific American Advocates programs, which are inclusive to various age groups, find and join your local chapter, or read released articles in their "Newsroom".
