SciLine

SciLine is a free service for journalists and scientists based at the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS), the worldâ€™s largest multidisciplinary scientific society. Editorially independent, nonpartisan, and funded by philanthropies, SciLine has a singular mission of enhancing the amount and quality of scientific evidence in news stories. Accuracy, clarity, fairness, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to evidence are core to that mission.

Quick Facts are concise summaries of newsworthy, science-related issues, designed to be easily scanned by journalists. Each page has been rigorously researched by SciLine staff and then reviewed by multiple independent experts, so journalists can insert selected facts into their stories with confidence. SciLine has a Quick Facts series about climate change, consisting of nine subtopics such as environmental justice and wildfires .
