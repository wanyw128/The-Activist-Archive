The Archive's Mission

"I alone cannot change the world, but I can cast a stone across the waters to create many ripples."

- Mother Theresa

The archive is a collection of global emergency resources and information. It strives to encourage activism and education about social issues and movements happening around the world. No matter where these conflicts are present, we believe that a strong and informed community can bring the change that our world and people need.

The Activist Archive provides resources and timelines of movements to promote education about and support to those who are victims of current crises.
