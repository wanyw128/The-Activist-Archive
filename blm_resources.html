Black Lives Matter

The Black Lives Matter organization provides free news and highlights important resources and events for BLM allies. Learn about Juneteenth, #BlackLoveLetters, and toolkits for organizers of activist or awareness events.

The Bail Project

The Bail Project fights mass incarceration and racial disparities in the bail system by helping to fund bails and reunite families. This project has chains in various states of the U.S. and celebrates nationwide contributions and impact. Explore the history of the bail systems and donate to the freedom of those in pretrial detention.

The Liberty Fund

The Liberty Fund mission is to “decarcerate” and support NYC residents who are subjected to bails they cannot afford. Through donations and their citywide programs, the Charitable Bail Program and the ROR (Released On Recognizance) Program, the Liberty Fund has maintained a significant impact in mitigating NYC’s jail population. Through this website, viewers are able to find resources and learn more about the unfair bail system, donate, or fundraise for the Liberty Fund.

Justice for Breonna Taylor

Justice for Breonna Taylor is a website dedicated to encouraging viewers to sign a petition urging authorities to bear responsibility of taking the life of Breonna Taylor.

1619 - Podcast from New York Times

“1619” is a New York Times audio series, hosted by Nikole Hannah-Jones, that examines the long shadow of American slavery. Listen to the episodes below, or read the transcripts by clicking the icon to the right of the play bar.

Justice for Belly Mujinga

This petition’s mission is calling for the Prime Minister to establish a public inquiry as to the circumstances that led to the death of Belly Mujinga, a worker at Govia Thameslink Railway who was assaulted at work by a man who claimed to have COVID-19 and later died of the virus.
